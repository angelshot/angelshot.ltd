Our mission is to provide tools and knowledge that empower women to safeguard themselves and encourage communities to act as havens for them.

Our Goals

Increase community engagement & accountability

51% of student rape and sexual assault victimizations occurred while the victim was pursuing leisure activities away from home.

89% of college campuses reported zero incidents of rape in 2016.

Encourage victims to report incidents and seek help

80% of rape and sexual assault victimizations of students and 67% of nonstudents go unreported to police.

Fewer than 1 in 5 female victims of rape and sexual assault received assistance from a victim services agency.

Prevent violence and ending "Rape Culture"

For both college students and nonstudents, the offender was known to the victim in about 80% of rape and sexual assault victimizations.

About a quarter of victims who did not report to police believed the incident was a personal matter, and 1 in 5 stated a fear of reprisal.
